## Notes

1. **Variants**: The wiki list includes many weapon variants (e.g., SCAR-H FDE, SCAR-H LB, SCAR-H X-17) that we don't have separately. We may want to add these as separate entries.

2. **Naming Differences**: Some weapons might be the same but named differently:
   - Our "M700" = Wiki "Remington Model 700"
   - Our "M4A1" = Wiki "Colt M4A1 5.56x45 assault rifle Carbine"
   - Our "MDR .308" = Wiki "Desert Tech MDR 7.62x51 assault rifle"
   - Our "MDR 5.56x45" = Wiki "Desert Tech MDR 5.56x45 assault rifle"

3. **Missing Categories**: We're missing several weapon categories:
   - Grenade launchers
   - Rocket launchers
   - Signal pistols
   - Many weapon variants

4. **Recommendation**: We should add the missing weapons, especially:
   - Popular variants (SCAR variants, M4A1 variants)
   - Missing SMGs (UZI, MPX, Vector)
   - Missing pistols (Desert Eagle variants, Glock 19X)
   - Missing shotguns (M3 Super 90, Auto Assault-12)
